# 🚀 AI Adoption Level and Workforce Dynamics Analysis

## 📌 Objective

To understand how AI adoption is influencing employees, job roles, salaries, automation risk, and workforce adaptability across industries using Data Analytics and Visualization techniques.

---

This project analyzes the impact of Artificial Intelligence adoption on workforce dynamics using Python and Data Analytics techniques. The analysis focuses on salary changes, job transformation, automation risk, and upskilling trends across different AI adoption levels.

# 📈 Key Insights

* Higher AI adoption levels show significant workforce transformation.
* AI is modifying many job roles rather than completely replacing them.
* Employees with upskilling opportunities show lower job replacement risk.
* Salary variations are noticeable across different AI adoption levels.
* Certain job categories face higher automation exposure.
* Workforce adaptation becomes stronger with continuous learning and AI integration.
* Visual analysis reveals clear relationships between AI adoption, automation risk, and employee outcomes.
